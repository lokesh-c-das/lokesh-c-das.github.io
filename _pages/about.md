I am an Assistant Professor in the [School of Computing](https://www.wichita.edu/academics/engineering/SoC/) at Wichita State University where I direct the <span style="color:#FF5349">AIMS-Artificial Intelligence, Mobility & Safety research Lab</span>. I received my PhD in Computer Science from the [University of Memphis](https://www.memphis.edu/), advised by [Dr. Myounggyu Won](https://www.memphis.edu/cs/people/faculty_pages/myounggyu-won.php). I obtained my MS in Computer Science from the University of Memphis in 2022 and my B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering from the [University of Dhaka](https://www.du.ac.bd/) Bangladesh, respectively. Before joining the Ph.D. program, I worked as a software engineer at [Samsung R&D Institute Bangladesh](https://research.samsung.com/srbd) and as a Senior Officer in the Information and Communication Technology Division at [Agrani Bank PLC](https://www.agranibank.org/). My research interests are Intelligent Transportation Systems, Connected and Autonomous Vehicles, V2X Communication, Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, Speech Emotion Recognition, Cyber Security, Internet of Things (IoT).
