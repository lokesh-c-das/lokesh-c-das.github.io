Welcome

I am an Assistant Professor (tenure track) in the School of Computing at Wichita State University. I received my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Memphis, advised by Dr. Myounggyu Won. I obtained my MS in Computer Science from the University of Memphis in 2022 and my B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering from the University of Dhaka, Bangladesh, respectively. Before joining the Ph.D. program, I worked as a software engineer at Samsung R&D Institute Bangladesh and as a Senior Officer in the Information and Communication Technology Division at Agrani Bank PLC. My research interests are Intelligent Transportation Systems, Connected and Autonomous Vehicles, Self-Driving Cars, Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, and Speech Emotion Recognition.
